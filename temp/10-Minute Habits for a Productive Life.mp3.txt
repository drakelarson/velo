Benefits of reducing screen time Taking regular breaks from screens during the workday can profoundly impact your focus, stress levels, and overall well-being. Here's how. 1. Improved focus and productivity.
 How it helps. Reducing screen time minimizes distractions, allowing you to concentrate on one task at a time. Example. Instead of checking emails every 10 minutes, set specific times to review and respond to messages. 2. Reduced stress and anxiety. How it helps. Limiting screen time gives your brain a break from constant stimulation,
 when you feel calmer and more in control. Example. Replace scrolling through social media during breaks with a short walk or mindfulness exercise.
